## Design Context

### Users
Johan Guse, a Design Engineer from Brazil. The audience is potential clients, employers, and peers in the design/engineering space — people who can judge craft. They visit to understand whether Johan thinks in both design and engineering terms, not just one.

### Brand Personality
Creative, Curious, Fast, Design Engineer.
The site must communicate "this person built this intentionally" — not a template, not a starter kit. Someone who moves between Figma and a terminal without switching gears. Warm and personal, not cold and corporate.

### Aesthetic Direction
Bold and expressive dark mode. Deep violet-tinted dark background with warm amber accent. Bricolage Grotesque for display (weight contrast for drama), Barlow for body. Full-bleed hero with PixelBackground canvas animation, TiltCard 3D project showcase, TextReveal scroll animations.

Anti-reference: current site — too simple, no emotion, looks like a Tailwind template, doesn't communicate Design Engineer.

### Design Principles
1. Typography does the heavy lifting — extreme weight/scale contrast communicates duality
2. Every interaction should feel engineered, not accidental
3. Dark by default, but light mode works too
4. The hero sets the tone — if it doesn't stop someone mid-scroll, start over
5. Personal voice in copy, not portfolio-speak
